# Check-in your guests

Welcome to our RSVP Event SaaS platform! This guide will walk you through the process of checking in your guests at your event step by step.

## Step 1: Navigate to the Event Management Page
1. Go to **My Events** page.
2. Click on the **Manage Event** button, present on the each events.
3. You will be redirected to the event management page.

## Step2: Help checkin your guests
1. Go to **Guests** section.
2. Click on **Check In Guests** option.
3. You will be redirected to verify page.
4. There are two options available to check in your guests:
    - **Scan QR Code**: Use this option to scan the QR code of the guest. Once scanned, the guest will be checked in automatically.
    - **Ticket Number**: Use this option to enter the ticket number of the guest. Once entered, and click on **Search attendee** button.
         - If the ticket number is valid, the guest will be checked in automatically.
         - If the ticket number is invalid, an error message will be displayed as "Attendee not found".
